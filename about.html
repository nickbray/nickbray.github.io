ABOUT

I'm Nick Bray, and i'm an Illustrator, Animator, Character Designer and 3D generalist from Sydney, Australia.

My personal work is heavily based around a love for character, humour, storytelling, and creative worldbuilding, accompanied by my own passion for developing my technical skills and learning new things.

As a result, i'm constantly producing new work and switching mediums, meaning that I've developed a lot of practical knowledge in many areas, such as Painting, Drawing, Animation, Modelling, Rigging, Lighting, Compositing, Sound Design, Photography, Video Editing, and Motion Graphics.

(Even the website you're looking at right now, I learned to design and build myself. Because i'm an egregious nerd.)

I'm also about to graduate from the B. Design in Animation course at the University of Technology, Sydney.

Please take a look at some of my work!
