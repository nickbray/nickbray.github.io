A 2D animated short film created as an assignment on a tight timeframe at UTS. The prompt we were given was "building a bridge" and we were challenged to tell a short narrative based off of that. The brief called for using royalty-free sound libraries as the sound design.

Animation and backgrounds were primarily accomplished in Photoshop, with some auxillary animation and compositing in After Effects.
